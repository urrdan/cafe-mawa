Welcome to Café Mawa.

Café Mawa will be a coffee house / café located in any Market Square Plaza/Shopping centers type of location in the Twin Cities area. The café will serve gourmet coffees, espresso and drip coffee, lattes, and smoothies, Beignets, Samosa, etc. The simple pastry offerings may vary with seasonality but the primary line will be Beignets, Samosa, breads, cookies, scones, and rolls. Some pastries will be supplied daily by a local bakery. The café will be owned and operated by Jay Aden, an entrepreneur with several years of experience running and managing chain restaurants. The café will be open for business Monday – Sunday 7am-10pm. Schedules are subject to changes depending on the area.
